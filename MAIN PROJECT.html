Dashavatar:

Dashavatar is the all-encompassing epic that celebrates the recurring triumph of good over evil – tenfold! The 9 avatars so far are Matsya (fish), Kurma (tortoise), Varaha (boar), Narasimha (half-man, half-lion), Vamana (dwarf man), Parshurama (man with an axe), Rama (moral man), Krishna (philosophical man). The ninth avatar is gautam buddha. The tenth avatar, Kalki is yet to manifest. The explanation of all the dashavatr is given below:
